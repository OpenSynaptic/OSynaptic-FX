# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the OSynapptic-FX community and its spaces will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Respecting privacy and confidentiality
- Contributing thoughtfully to discussions

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, insulting or derogatory comments
- Personal attacks of any kind
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, deliberate misinformation, or bad-faith arguments
- Discrimination based on personal characteristics
- Intimidation or threats, online or offline
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct which would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct. Maintainers who do not follow the Code of Conduct may be temporarily or permanently removed from the project.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:

- The GitHub repository
- Project issues and discussions
- Code reviews and pull requests
- Any official project communication channels

## Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

1. A private warning from project maintainers
2. Temporary suspension of contribution privileges
3. Permanent removal from the project
